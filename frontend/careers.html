Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information including:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone)
  • Payment information
  • Travel preferences and booking history
  • Device and usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process transactions and provide services
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Communicate important service updates
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share information with:

  • Legal authorities when required by law

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us improve our services
  • Marketing Cookies: Show relevant advertisements

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use services like Google Analytics that may set their own cookies.
